About

Luminary is a battle calculator for Eclipse: Second Dawn for the Galaxy.

Targeting

Luminary assumes that both AI and player ships follow the targeting rules for AI ships. This is not always optimal and as such you should take results with a grain of salt. Note that this is the strategy employed by all other Eclipse calculators as far as I know.
